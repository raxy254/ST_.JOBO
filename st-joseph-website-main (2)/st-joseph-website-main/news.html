January 2025 Academics

Outstanding KCSE Performance

St. Joseph's Boys High School Kitale continues its tradition of academic excellence with outstanding KCSE results in 2025. The school achieved a mean score of 9.7 (B+), ranking top in Trans Nzoia County. Over 95% of our students qualified for university placement, with many securing places in prestigious institutions both locally and internationally.
