About St. Joseph's Boys Kitale

Building tomorrow's leaders through holistic education grounded in faith, discipline, and academic excellence.

St. Joseph's Boys High School Kitale is a leading national boys' boarding school located in Kitale, Trans Nzoia County, Kenya. Founded in 1952 under Catholic sponsorship, the school has grown into one of the most respected academic institutions in the country. It sits on a 75-acre campus and continues to produce disciplined, high-performing graduates prepared for university and leadership roles.

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond academics, encompassing character formation, spiritual growth, and co-curricular engagement. We prepare young men to become responsible citizens and future leaders who will make meaningful contributions to society.

Admissions

Admission into the school is through national placement based on academic performance. The school offers a fully boarding environment designed to support academic and personal growth.

Admission Requirements

  • Strong KCPE/KJSEA performance
  • Placement through Ministry of Education
  • Commitment to school values and discipline
  • Medical fitness certification
